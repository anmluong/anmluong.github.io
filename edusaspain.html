EducationUSA Spain

Assets created for EducationUSA Spain's site and social media

what it is

EducationUSA Spain Competitive College Club (CCC) guides and supports students in Spain through the admissions process for U.S. universities.

what i did

I built upon colors from EducationUSA's styling guidelines and introduced brighter colors to create illustrations that lightened and brightened the formal feel of the official branding. These illustrations were then used on the EducationUSA Spain's website, which I helped create and launch at the beginning of 2019, and their Instagram page.
