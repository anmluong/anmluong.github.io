About me

How I got into design

Growing up, I was obsessed with art but ended up diving deeper into my STEM classes as I got into high school and landed in the engineering world in college. When I realized I couldn't see myself in strictly technical roles, I took some steps back towards my design-oriented roots while keeping my technical knowledge in my toolkit.

Starting with web design, I poked through and inspected others' sites to learn basic HTML/CSS. I then got a taste of human-centered design through a hackathon and picked up Figma, which led me to a UX design course where I fell in love with how UX connected people and tech. Since then, I've continued learning new skills and tools as I go.
