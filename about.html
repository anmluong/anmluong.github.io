About me

as a person...

I've always been driven and scrappy. When I wasn't allowed to buy a friendship bracelet-making wheel in elementary school, I made my own loom out of a cereal box. When duct tape was trendy, I made and sold duct tape wallets with plastic ID pockets. I find ways to get things done, even if it means creating a makeshift field hockey stick out of cardboard boxes and rulers.

as a student & designer...

I want to use what I learn to help others. I'm a Systems Engineering major at UVA minoring in Computer Science and Global Studies in Education. In the digital design space, I'm able to combine my visual eye with analytical skills from Systems Engineering, technical knowledge from CS, and empathy and listening skills from mentoring and multicultural education courses.
